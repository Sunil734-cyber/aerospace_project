## HOW TO USE:

1. Go to **gamma.app**
2. Click **"Create New Presentation"** or **"New"**
3. Select **"PowerPoint"** as format
4. Paste the entire prompt above into the prompt box
5. Click **"Generate"** or **"Create"**
6. Wait for Gamma to generate the presentation (usually 1-2 minutes)
7. Download and customize as needed
